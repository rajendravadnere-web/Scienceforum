Objectives (Section 2 of Deed)

  • 2.1: Inculcate scientific temper by questioning and using scientific methods, avoiding dogmas.
  • 2.2: Develop interests in science content.
  • 2.3: Facilitate discussions on current developments in science, technology, and their impacts.
  • 2.4: Foster curiosity and joyful learning to propagate scientific thinking.
  • 2.5: Assist in solving daily problems systematically, creatively, and sustainably.
  • 2.6: Use ICT for cost-effective achievement of objectives.
